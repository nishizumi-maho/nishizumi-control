## Purpose and Intended Use

Dominant Control for iRacing is designed and provided exclusively for:

1. **Accessibility Assistance**: To help drivers with physical disabilities or limitations access vehicle controls they cannot easily reach or manipulate
2. **Equipment Compensation**: To provide control access for users whose equipment lacks certain features (rotary knobs, button boxes, etc.)
3. **Educational Purposes**: To learn about telemetry systems, API integration, and vehicle dynamics in a learning environment

**This software is NOT intended for competitive racing or to provide unfair advantages.**
